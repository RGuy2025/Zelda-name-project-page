The Many Faces of Zelda

Throughout history there have been many notable people with the name Zelda. Most people associate the name with the video game character but in fact that character was named after Zelda Fitzgerald!

Other notable Zelda's are Zelda Williams, daughter of the late great Robin Williams (named after the video game) and Marilyn Monroe used the pseudonym Zelda Zonk!
